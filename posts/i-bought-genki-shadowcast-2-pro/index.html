Genki ShadowCast 2 Pro

Somehow I came across a link to Kickstarter campaign of Genki ShadowCast 2 Pro. It's a device that allows you to stream your gameplay from Nintendo Switch (my option), PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and other consoles to your computer or whatever has an HDMI output.

I bought a kit with Shadow Cast 2 Pro, Shadow Cast 2, Covert Dock 2. I think for my needs simple Shadow Cast 2 Pro would be more than enough.
